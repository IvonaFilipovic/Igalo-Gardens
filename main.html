About the

Complex

The IGALO GARDENS COMPLEX is planning to present the local environment in its finest light by planting palm trees, subtropical plants and other Mediterranean vegetation. Within the complex there are many arranged walking paths, parks with greenery and rest places, as well as a children's playground. There are also 7 more swimming pools, including 1 for children. To the left of the complex, a panoramic lift is planned to reach the highest point of the complex. In order to reduce exhaust emissions, transport within the complex, as well as to the beach, will take place by electric cars. The complex would lease a part of the beach in front of the Simo Milosevic Institute, and for its guests would also provide its own transportation to the most beautiful beaches on Zanjac and Miriste.

Welcome to

Montenegro

AN UP-AND-COMING YACHTING PARADISE, this fascinating country rewards travellers with charming towns, challenging hiking paths, deep rafting canyons and smooth fords. A high number of sunny days in summer and deep snowfall in winter determine the two most developed forms of tourism in Montenegro: the coastal direction in summer and recreational routes during the winter. There are several mountain resorts here which offer visitors a great many opportunities for adventure, whether it be the slopes or cross-country skiing, snowboarding, mountain biking, hiking, riding a ski-doo or snowshoeing.

Our convenient

Location

IGALO IS LOCATED IN A UNIQUE CLIMATE ZONE, where 2 air streams meet and combine, both Mediterranean and subtropical. The summers are warm, with over 200 sunny days in a year, and the winters are typically dry, a fact which contributes to an average temperature of 16.2°C. Thanks to the uniqueness of the climate, it is expected that a special programme of Thalassotherapy will be introduced, with its ample applications for treating chronic rheumatic problems, respiratory illnesses and cardiovascular disease, as well as skin conditions.

Improve your

Health

Igalo is known as a tourist resort, and thanks to the healing - sea mud, is the centre of health tourism in Montenegro. It is also known for its mineral water called Igaljka. Igalo is also known as an air spa. From Mount Orjen, winds blow and in Igalo they mix with the sea air. This kind of air has a beneficial effect on the human respiratory system. Due to the large number of sunny days and the mild climate, it is possible to enjoy Montenegro all year round. Spend time walking or cycling along the 7 km long coastline.
