The Igalo Gardens complex is a unique combination of all the elements of health and tourism.

Due to its mild climate, it is suitable for older people to stay and walk all year round. For more endurance, other activities are possible, such as cycling and canoeing. For fans of extreme sports, there is rafting on the river Tara, which is 180 km away. During the winter, it is possible to reach the ski centre Žabljak by car for 2.5 hours and enjoy winter sports there.

Institut Simo Milosevic

In the 1930s, the Igalo mud was tested in the laboratory of the famous French spa Vichy, which confirmed its exceptional healing properties. After the Second World War, the spa "Institute Simo Milosevic" (igalospa.com) was established in Igalo.The Institute for Physical Medicine, Rehabilitatiton and Rheumatology „Dr Simo Milosevic“ JSC Igalo is considered to be one of the largest and most famous institutions for multidisciplinary spa treatment on the Balkans. It is a pioneer of the modern physical and preventive medicine, rehabilitation, thalassotherapy and wellness, and became one of the leading international centers for rehabilitation of children, adults and seniors.
