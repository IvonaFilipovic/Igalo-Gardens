The Igalo Gardens complex is a unique combination of all the elements of health and tourism.

Igalo is known as a tourist resort, and thanks to the healing - sea mud from Blatna plaza, is the centre of health tourism in Montenegro. It is also known for its mineral water called Igaljka. In the 1930s, the Igalo mud was tested in the laboratory of the famous French spa Vichy, which confirmed its exceptional healing properties. After the Second World War, the spa "Institute Simo Milosevic" (igalospa.com) was established in Igalo. Igalo is also known as an air spa. From Mount Orjen, winds blow and in Igalo they mix with the sea air. This kind of air has a beneficial effect on the human respiratory system. Due to the large number of sunny days and the mild climate, it is possible to enjoy Montenegro all year round. Spend time walking or cycling along the 7 km long coastline.

Institut Simo Milosevic

The Institute for Physical Medicine, Rehabilitatiton and Rheumatology „Dr Simo Milosevic“ JSC Igalo is considered to be one of the largest and most famous institutions for multidisciplinary spa treatment on the Balkans. It is a pioneer of the modern physical and preventive medicine, rehabilitation, thalassotherapy and wellness, and became one of the leading international centers for rehabilitation of children, adults and seniors.
